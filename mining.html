Mining

Kevacoin uses Proof-of-Work as its consensus method. The hashing algorithmn is Cryptonight V8 (variant 2). Cryptonight based cryptocurrencies (e.g. monero, bytecoin) have different header structure than Bitcoin/Litecoin. Therefore, the existing Cryptonight miners (e.g. XMRig) are incompatible with Kevacoin. We provide reference implementations for CPU, AMD and Nvidia GPU based on ccminer.

Mining Pools / Solo Mining

We provide a reference implementation of Kevacoin mining pool based on NOMP(Node Open Ming Portal). You can also use it for solo mining by running it on your local machine and connect your miner to it.
